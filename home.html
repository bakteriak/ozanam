Welcome to Ozanam Daycare

Who we are

Established in 1988 Ozanam Daycare is registered with the Southern Health & Social Care Trust as a 120 place childcare facility for children in the 0-12 year age range. We are members of Early Years, the largest organisation in Northern Ireland for young Children, which aims to promote high quality childcare for children aged 0 - 12 and their families.

Our Beliefs

Children learn and develop in a range of different ways, and at different rates. At Ozanam, we recognise the need for learning and development in all areas.

We believe that every child is unique, having their own likes, dislikes and capabilities. We offer support to those who may be struggling and seek to encourage those who are advanced.

We firmly believe in fostering postive relationships, as children learn and develop through loving and secure relationships.

We also believe that the environment plays a vital role in promoting supporting and extending children’s development and learning.

Our Aims

Ozanam seeks to provide a professional, quality daycare service at an affordable price. We aim to encourage children to develop through social interaction with their peers.

We Encourage use of our facility by all sections of the community and provide employment and training opportunities for staff.

We aim to provide a programme of activities which will stimulate, educate and amuse children in our care
